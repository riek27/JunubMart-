Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does delivery take?

We offer same-day delivery within Juba for orders placed before 2 PM. For areas outside Juba, delivery typically takes 2-3 business days.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, mobile money transfers, and cash on delivery for your convenience.

Can I return a product?

Yes, we have a 14-day return policy for most items. Products must be in original condition with all packaging.

Do you offer technical support?

Yes, we provide technical support for all electronics purchased from us. Contact our support team for assistance.
