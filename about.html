About JunubMart

Learn about our journey, values, and commitment to serving South Sudan

Our Story

JunubMart was founded in 2020 with a vision to revolutionize online shopping in South Sudan. We understand the unique challenges and opportunities in our market and are committed to providing solutions that make life easier for our customers.

Our journey began with a simple observation: South Sudanese consumers deserved better access to quality products without the hassle of traditional shopping. We set out to create an e-commerce platform that combines convenience, reliability, and exceptional customer service.

Today, JunubMart has grown into a trusted name in Juba and beyond, serving thousands of satisfied customers with a wide range of products from electronics to fashion, home goods to educational materials.

Our Mission & Vision

Driving progress through innovative e-commerce solutions

Our Mission

To provide convenient access to quality products through innovative e-commerce solutions that empower communities and drive economic growth in South Sudan. We strive to bridge the gap between local consumers and global markets while supporting local businesses and entrepreneurs.

Our Vision

To become the leading online marketplace in South Sudan, known for reliability, quality, and exceptional customer service. We envision a future where every South Sudanese citizen has equal access to the products they need to thrive, regardless of their location.
