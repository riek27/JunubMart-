About JunubMart

Learn more about our story, mission, and vision

Our Story

JunubMart was founded in 2020 with a vision to revolutionize online shopping in South Sudan. What started as a small startup has grown into Juba's premier online marketplace, serving thousands of satisfied customers.

We recognized the need for a reliable, convenient shopping platform that brings quality products directly to your doorstep, saving you time and effort.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make quality products accessible to everyone in South Sudan through a seamless online shopping experience. We're committed to bridging the gap between consumers and quality goods.

We strive to support local businesses while bringing international products to our customers, creating a diverse marketplace that meets all your needs.

Our Vision

We envision a future where shopping in South Sudan is convenient, reliable, and accessible to all. We aim to be the leading e-commerce platform that empowers both consumers and local businesses.

Through innovation and customer-centric services, we're building a shopping ecosystem that contributes to South Sudan's economic growth and digital transformation.

Our Values

Customer First

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We listen to your needs and continuously improve our services to exceed your expectations.

Quality Assurance

We carefully select and vet all products to ensure they meet our high standards for quality and reliability.

Community Focus

We're committed to supporting local businesses and contributing to the growth of South Sudan's economy.
